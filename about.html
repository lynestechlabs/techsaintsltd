About us

O ur company was founded on the belief that navigating the tech world can be a daunting task, especially with the abundance of advertisements and the prevalence of fake products.

We were inspired by stories of individuals who broke into the tech industry via non-traditional methods and backgrounds

  • 1. We wanted to create a platform that would guide people through the maze of the tech world and help them distinguish between genuine, original products and fake ones. We were also inspired by the story of Mark Rober, who created a company that aimed to inspire people all over the world to love science and change the way they think about it
  • 2. We believe that technology has the power to change the world for the better, and we want to help people harness that power. Our goal is to provide our customers with the knowledge and tools they need to succeed in the tech world. We are inspired by the success stories of companies like Pinterest, whose inventor, Ben Silbermann, clung to his idea and battled to make it work
  • 3. We hope to inspire our customers to pursue their dreams and achieve success in the tech industry.
